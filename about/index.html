My Story

  • 1983

    Born in Da-Xing-An-Ling

    Nearly the most north-east place in China, I was born in that winter with huge snow. So my name is "东"(east) and "冬"(winter)

  • 1996

    High School time in Dalian

    I got my first computer in my high school, and lost into it. I learnt VF/VB/HTML/ASP/SQL myself, and played with programming and website. My first works "Dongdong Player" was published in PCHOME.NET, and "Windows Cleaner" was recommend by PC-FAN magazine in 1999.

  • 2000

    Growing with Internet

    My first site "Wallpaper Collection" was built in 1998, the earliest generation in Chinese internet. After that I operated several sites built by my self after class, and earned my first pot of gold about 20,000 RMB. My favorite navigation site and picture collection site have touched 300,000 PV per day in 2001.

  • 2002

    Only Computer in College

    I filed all computer major on candidate colleges, not good one finally, but more important I realized that interest is the teacher. I have got scholarship 3 times, all "A"s grade in computer experiment course. Startup computer association with roommates, and having hundred members, we organized many activities and trainings.

  • Coding is More

    Besides the coding classwork, I participated many programming competitions online, like ACM/ICPC, Topcoder, Baidu Astar... Gained some nice T-shirts also with my C/C++ coding skill.

    Harder in Huawei

I love to kown about that, love coding, love internet, love solving problem with computer... It also makes me better, makes the world better.
